Our Products

Any Time Check (ATC) is a non-invasive device for measurement of blood glucose level. It has been designed so as to enable diabetic people to monitor their blood glucose on a daily basis, without having to go through the pain of pricking themselves every time. It will also assist in mass screening of diabetes and provide motivation to the users so that they continue putting in efforts required to control their blood sugar levels in the acceptable range.
